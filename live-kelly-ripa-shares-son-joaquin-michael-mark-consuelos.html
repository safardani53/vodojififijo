    LIVE with Kelly & Mark star Kelly Ripa has honored her and her husband, Mark Consuelos', sons in a new post on social media.

    Kelly's recent photo dump on her Instagram account spotlighted the Conseulos kids, Michael and Joaquin, throughout the years.

    The carousel post included individual photos of Michael, 26, and Joaquin, 20, as toddlers as well as adult men.

    There were also snaps of the brothers with their mom, Kelly, 52, and their dad Mark, 52.

    Kelly made sure to include an equal number of images of Mark with their boys while they were kids and ones when they were all grown up.

    The All My Children alum shared all of the family photos because of the national holiday that is happening today, National Sons Day.

    WE'VE GOT A LIVE! ONE

    Michael was born on June 2, 1997, and Joaquin was born on February 24, 2003.

    Michael attended New York University from 2016 to 2020 and is now an actor, writer, and director who has appeared in multiple films and television series.

    According to his IMDb page, Michael has been featured in the shows Duck Dodgers, Go, Diego! Go!, and Riverdale.

    He also appeared in the 2012 short, The Bensonhurst Spelling Bee, and in the 2022 movie, Let's Get Physical.

    Joaquin is currently a student at the University of Michigan.

    He is enrolled in the college's School of Music, Theatre and Dance, with a major in theatre performance.

    Kelly and Mark's third child, Lola Consuelos, 22, recently graduated from New York University, and is pursuing music.
