    She’s not like a regular mom — Chrissy Teigen is a cool mom! The Sports Illustrated model, 30, shared a couple of Snapchat videos of herself playing around with baby Luna on Friday, April 22.

    The first-time parent, who welcomed her daughter with husband John Legend on April 14, played with the puppy, glitter makeup and flower crown filters while holding her sleeping mini-me close to her chest. 

    Using Snapchat’s popular puppy filter, the 30-year-old licked her little girl’s head. She later joked about her new makeup-free life using the glitter face filter. 

    “I don’t remember what makeup feels like,” Teigen captioned the snap.

    Baby Luna made her social media debut just five days after being born.
