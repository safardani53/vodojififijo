    04 Aug 2024, 00:00

    ALEX SCOTT stunned on the red carpet as she arrived at the BBC Sports Personality of the Year awards.

    The former England footballer, 39, presented this evening's show alongside Gary Lineker, Gabby Logan and Clare Balding.

    It was the 70th edition of the annual ceremony celebrating the year in sport and heavy favourite Mary Earps got her hands on the iconic trophy after the public vote.

    But ahead of the main event, Scott stole the show with her amazing outfit.

    Braless Scott rocked an incredible full-length, long-sleeve red gown with an open cutaway chest and a collar.

    And she completed the look with red shoes as she posed for photos - including alongside her fellow hosts.

    Scott's girlfriend Jess Glynne joined her at the gala to support her from the VIP seats.

    And the popstar was even in the dressing room with her as they got ready - posting a selfie on Instagram in a rare social media photo together, a month after their red carpet debut.

    Fans could not get enough of Scott's vibrant choice.

    One said: "Another day, another red carpet event, another slay from Alex Scott."

    Another wrote: "Wow."

    A third added: "You look fabulous."

    Scott started as a regular at the SPOTY gala in 2020 - one of the highlights of the year for sports fans across the country.

    The ex-defender is a constant fixture on the BBC with her WSL coverage and presenting Football Focus - prompting a bitter spat with Joey Barton in recent weeks following his misogynistic Twitter rants.

    But she is no stranger to dressing up for galas - with a whole host of stunning outfits modelled by the Londoner over the years.

    The most daring of all was her braless shimmering see-through silver number at the GQ Awards in November.

    Scott was joined by the Lionesses at that particular gala - and the Women's World Cup finalists were back with her tonight at MediaCity in Salford.

    Earps arrived as the odds-on favourite and certainly made a splash with her lacy outfit while her fellow nominee Stuart Broad looked dapper in his black suit alongside partner Mollie King.
