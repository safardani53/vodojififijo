    And then there were four. As March Madness has turned into April Anarchy, the Hollywood Life Celebrity March Madness is racing towards the finish. Beyonce, 35, looks to finish off Selena Gomez, 24, while Taylor Swift, 27, takes on Emma Watson, 26. Is Queen Bey headed for an upset similar to UConn’s shocking loss to Mississippi State? Can Taylor pull off a UNC and defeat Emma?

    Only two of these fierce competitors will face off in the final, so it’s up to YOU to pick your favorites. Make your picks below and come back to see which queen will advance to the championship vote on April 7.

    Elite 8 March Madness — See Pics Of The March Madness Highlights

    Beyonce Vs. Selena Gomez

    It’s been smooth sailing for Queen Bey through this tournament. She dominated the vote with Khloe Kardashian, 32, before going on to defeat Nina Dobrev, 28, and Jennifer Lopez, 47, in the Sweet 16 and Elite 8. However, Bey hasn’t faced someone as popular (at least, in terms of this tournament) as Selena. While Bey’s 76% victory over Khloe is impressive, Selena managed to score 80% of the vote over Miley Cyrus, 24. Sel dominated the Battle of the Disney stars. She easily dispatched Nicki Minaj, 34, and Gigi Hadid, 21. Could Sel knock out Queen Bey en route to the crown?

    [interaction id=”58e3fc7147afc37b3b046d01″]

    Taylor Swift Vs. Emma Watson

    If Selena successfully sinks Beyonce, will it be a “Squad Vs. Squad” final? Taylor, arguably the No. 2 seed in the Real Conference, had the power of the vote behind her, as she surpassed Rihanna, 29, Kylie Jenner, 19, and Zayn Malik, 24, with relative ease.

    [interaction id=”58e3fd3d47afc37b3b046d05″]

    On the other side stands Emma, in what many could call the “Cinderella Story” (or in this case, the “Beauty & The Beast” story) of the tournament. Both Justin Bieber, 23, and Kanye West, 39, the No. 1 and No. 2 seeds of the Swag Conference, exited the competition early. Emma soared past Remy Ma, Milo Ventimigila, 39, and Liam Hemsworth, 27. Yet, Emma hasn’t faced anyone like Taylor in this competition. Will the Swifties put Tay in the final or will Emma’s magic keep the fairy tale going?

    Which two stars do you want to win, HollywoodLifers? Don’t forget to vote!
