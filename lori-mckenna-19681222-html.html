    Lorraine "Lori" McKenna is an American, American and country folk singer, songwriter and performer. In 2016, she was nominated for a Grammy Award for Song of the Year and Best Country Song award for co-writing the hit single "Girl Crush" performed by Little Big Town. In 2017, she won the Best Country Song at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards for writing "Humble and Kind" performed by Tim McGraw. McKenna along with Lady Gaga, Natalie Hemby and Hillary Lindsey wrote the second single from the soundtrack for the 2018 A Star Is Born titled "Always Remember Us This Way". McKenna performed her backing vocals with Lindsey and Hemby, and the song received a Song of the Year nomination at the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards.
