    Holiday Inn hotel transformed into concert venue with live gigs from your bedroom

    A HOLIDAY Inn hotel has transformed itself into a gig venue where families can watch live concerts from their hotel room. Guests staying at the Holiday Inn Gatwick were able to watch two 45-minute performances on stage from the comfort of their bedrooms.

    16 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A HOLIDAY Inn hotel has transformed itself into a gig venue where families can watch live concerts from their hotel room.

    Guests staying at the Holiday Inn Gatwick were able to watch two 45-minute performances on stage from the comfort of their bedrooms.

    Guests staying in rooms "with a view" bought two tickets to the show and an overnight stay for two people with breakfast and overnight car parking.

    The bands performed on stage in the enclosed courtyard of the hotel as guests watched from their large open bedroom windows.

    Pictures showed families watching a Queen tribute band and dancing in their rooms.

    Live From Your Bedroom launched this weekend with a calendar of live music events, performed on stage in the courtyard hotel in Crawley, West Sussex.

    Most read in News

    Chris Schoeman, hotel director, said: “Festivals have been cancelled, concerts have been postponed, theatres are still unable to open and now even cinemas are beginning to close.

    “So we want to give people a reason to come together, socially distanced of course, and make up for missed birthdays, anniversaries and other celebrations over the last few months, whilst also supporting other businesses and performers who have been without any income for a long time.

    “When booking our performers, they had informed us of how difficult it had been for them trying to find work recently, so we are thrilled that they are able to get back up on stage once again.”
