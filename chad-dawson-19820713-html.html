    Chad Dawson is an American professional boxer. He has held several world light championships and was one of the top boxers in that league from 2006 to 2013. Dawson became prominent on the world stage in 2007 , as he defeated Tomasz Adamek to become the WBC lightweight champion. After canceling that title, he defeated Antonio Tarver in 2008 to win the IBF and IBO titles, and beat him in a rematch in 2009. Dawson's first career failure was a loss to Jean Pascal in 2010. He would recover by winning the WBC title for the second time, as well as Ring magazine and linear titles, beating Bernard Hopkins in 2012. That same year, The Ring ranked Dawson as the tenth most active boxer in the world, in pounds. Then in 2012, Dawson was relegated to super-middleweight, a division he hasn't played in since 2006, to challenge Andre Ward for the unified WBA, WBC, Ring and Linear titles.
