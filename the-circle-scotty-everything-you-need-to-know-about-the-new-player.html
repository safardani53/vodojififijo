    Who is Scotty?

    Scotty is a 36-year-old former semi-professional footballer.

    Since hanging up his boots, the born and raised South Londoner became a black cab driver, which he still does now.

    This includes a lion, rose petal, wolf, swallows, Rising Sun, clock face and LOYALTY plastered across his mid-drift.

    Ibiza King is probably the nickname that Scotty lives for. Judging by his Instagram account, he spends a lot of time on the White Isle.

    Professional Footballer?

    Although Scotty played football full-time for the most part of his career, he never really hit the professional level.

    A journeyman of the English non-league system, he started his career at Sutton United in 1999.

    Scotty went on to play for the likes of Dulwich Town, Aldershot Town, Boreham Wood, Billericay Town and Grays Athletic, eventually finishing his career at Croydon in 2014.

    None of those teams played higher than four or five divisions below England’s top league.
