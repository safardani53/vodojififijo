    Inside Kandi Burruss $1.7 million Atlanta mansion featuring TEN baths and red crocodile-embossed

    10 Feb 2024, 00:00

    REAL Housewives of Atlanta's Kandi Burruss' 7 bedroom/9.5 bathroom home has an incredible piano room fit for the Grammy Award-winning artist.

    The southwest Atlanta $1.7M mansion's massive family room is also big enough for all the housewives to hang out and spill the tea.

    Kandi has taken viewers inside her beautiful home, which she shares with her husband Todd and their children, throughout the Bravo reality series.

    One of the Xscape member and mom's favorite spaces is the piano room.

    The gorgeous instrument is wrapped in red crocodile print.

    There are also pops of red throughout the music room and house.

    Kandi can be seen cuddling her two youngest babies, Ace and baby Blaze, in bright red chairs in the kitchen.

    The 43-year-old also has a huge living room to host all of her RHOA friends.

    It features a gigatic grey velvet tufted sofa with matching ottomans and a fuscia coffee table.

    The bold statement piece was actually a dining room table.

    But Kandi's interior designer cut it down to fit in this space.

    There are also bright pink pillows and pieces woven through the entire room to tie the color scheme together.

    When she's not in the family room, Kandi and her family spend a lot of time in the kitchen.

    It's where toddler Ace sometimes brushes his teeth before school.

    Kandi even commissioned a black diamond-like accent wall in one room.

    The home seems perfect for this busy bravolebrity.
