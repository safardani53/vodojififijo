    KHLOE Kardashian has shown off the elaborate Christmas decorations inside her mansion as she continues to compete with her sisters.

    The Hulu star recently took to her Instagram Stories to show off the over-the-top decor inside the dining room of her $17 million Hidden Hills mansion.

    Khloe, 39, panned her massive dining table that featured a full-length piece of garland going down the center.

    The gorgeous centerpiece was accompanied by various, dark-colored candles that were lit throughout.

    The Good American founder then gave a peak at another table that was pushed up against the wall, which was decorated with a similar garland.

    The long wreath of leaves was intertwined with twinkling white holiday lights and had a few decorative, shimmering Christmas trees placed around.

    Khloe's floor-to-ceiling Christmas tree could be seen in the background of the video, which was covered in sparkling white lights and neutral-toned ornaments.

    Next to the tree, which was placed in the corner of the room, were animatronic white reindeer that moved their heads up and down.

    In a following IG Story, the reality star credited Butterfly Floral for the decor, admitting she was now in: "Christmas heaven."

    Khloe showed off her impressive dining room in another video last week, which showed her daughter True, 5, dancing with her baby brother Tatum, one, and her cousin Dream, seven.
