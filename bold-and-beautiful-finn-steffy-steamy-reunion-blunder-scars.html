    THE Bold and the Beautiful fans have spotted a major blunder during Finn and Steffy's steamy reunion during Wednesday’s episode.

    While viewers have praised the show for giving them the NSFW scene, they couldn’t help but call out its “one big issue.”

    With Finn (Tanner Novlan) now revealed to be alive and finally reunited with his wife Steffy (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood), the two made sure to have proper alone time during Wednesday’s episode.

    After he got checked out by a doctor and was confirmed to be good to go to resume “regular activity,” he and Steffy wasted no time making their way to the bedroom.

    With the pair ripping off their clothes, viewers got their first look at the duo’s post-shooting bodies.

    While Bold and the Beautiful fans praised the scene for being extra streamy and just what they wanted, they couldn’t help but notice there seemed to be a blunder with the duo’s post-shooting bodies.
