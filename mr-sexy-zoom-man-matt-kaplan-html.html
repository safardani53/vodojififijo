    Mr Sexy Zoom Man became a mystery looming over the internet since Alexandra "Alex" Cooper introduced him as her new boyfriend on her famous Call Me Daddy podcast circa 2020. 

    Since the first time she talked about him, Cooper has gushed about her romantic life frequently but has never disclosed the identity of her partner.

    Instead, she has always used the nickname Mr. Sexy Zoom Man, typical of the podcaster who uses code names like Red Sox, Door #3, and Slim Shady to refer to her ex-boyfriends. 

    But the more Cooper maintains mystery around her personal life, her fans find more ways to demystify the puzzle, and they have already figured who might be the man behind the Zoom nickname. 

    Who Is Mr Sexy Zoom Man?

    Rumors are rife that Mr. Sexy Zoom Man is non-other than the famed Matthew Kaplan, otherwise known as Matt Kaplan. The speculations are based on two significant factors.

    Firstly, the celebrity host has revealed that her boyfriend is a movie producer.

    Guess what? Kaplan is also a movie producer with notable projects like Time Cut, Body Cam, Versus, To All the Boys I've Loved Before, and Are You Afraid of the Dark? under his belt.

    Matt Kaplan and Alex Cooper's Dog Henry Revealed It All

    The second major hint is Mr. Sexy Zoom Man's dog named Henry. Cooper has featured the pup on her socials multiple times and introduced the dog through her podcast.

    But that's not all. Cooper even got into controversy for licking the dog's tongue in June 2021. But squabbles aside, the exciting part is Henry is hugely related to Kaplan.

    Matt Kaplan and Mr. Sexy Zoom Man's pooch seem to be the same dog, Henry. (Photo: Hollywoodmask)

    On February 2021, Ace Entertainment posted a picture of Henry on Twitter and revealed that it was Matt Kaplan's dog. The update also mentioned that Henry was featured in Kaplan's movie To All the Boys.

    No doubt, the Henry in the post and the Henry on Cooper's social media accounts, whom she introduced as her boyfriend's pet, are the same. 

    But despite the substantial evidence, no precise conclusion can be drawn on the identity of Cooper's new beau â€” unless she or her boyfriend, possibly Kaplan, break the silence on the matter. 

    As for Kaplan's IMDb page, the site was quick to announce that Kaplan has been married to Alexandra Cooper since February 3, 2021, which just so happens to be the day that Cooper put out a tweet to announce that she had a boyfriend for real.

    The Meaning Behind Mr. Sexy Zoom Man 

    Cooper has a reasonable explanation as to why she named her boyfriend "Sexy Zoom Man." According to her accounts, she met her partner on Zoom for business amid the pandemic for the first time. 

    The 27-year-old was swooned by his personality right at the moment. Soon, the pair met for dinner in Los Angeles, her boyfriend's hometown, and instantly hit it off. 

    In an interview with Bustle in July 2021, Cooper admitted that her new beau was far better than the rest of her ex-boyfriends.

    The Boston University alum elaborated he respected her career choice and understood the reason behind the privacy, which had been a significant problem in all her previous relationships.

    She also shared that she would continue talking about their love life in her podcast while avoiding identifying her partner as long as possible. "It's interesting," she added at the end of the topic.
