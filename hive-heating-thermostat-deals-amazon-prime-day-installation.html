    EMBRACE the smart home revolution with this cracking deal on a Hive Active Heating system.

    For Prime Day 2018, Hive has become one of the best Amazon deals around, with the smart home heating gear dropping to below £150 – including professional installation.

    Hive Active Heating doesn't come cheap: typically, you'd expect to pay £249 for the set-up, although Amazon's usual price is closer to £224.80.

    But Prime Day deals have brought the price crashing down to a lowly £149.99.

    That's a 33% saving on Amazon's usual price, which works out at exactly £74.81 extra in your pocket.

    It's also the lowest price that Amazon ever sold this particular product for, so if you've been weighing up getting Hive, now's your chance.

    • Hive Active Heating with Professional Installation at Amazon for £149.99 on Prime Day – buy now

    Hive Active Heating is a home-wide system that gives you smart control over your heating and hot water.

    You can control the temperature of your house (and your tap water) all through an app.

    Better still, the app is available on your smartphone, tablet or laptop – and there's support for the Amazon Echo's Alexa voice-controlled assistant, too.

    You can set heating and hot water schedules, with up to six daily time slots. And you can use the automatic frost protection feature to prevent frozen pipes.

    Hive is smart, too – it'll use your smartphone location to work out whether you're at home or not.

    That way, it can start heating your home for when you return, and cool things down once you've left.

    There's also a Holiday Mode, which will heat your home automatically so that it's warm when you get back.

    It's a little tricky to set up, so this particularly deal is exciting because it comes with professional installation.

    That means you can kick back and let someone else sort out the set-up for you.

    And once it's done, you'll be able to enjoy smart home tech that feels like it's from the future.
