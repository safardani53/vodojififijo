    THE TODAY Show host Jenna Bush Hager has replaced Savannah Guthrie on the panel.

    This comes after Savannah flew to the U.K. to cover Queen Elizabeth II's passing.

    On Monday's show, Jenna took a seat behind the main panel desk.

    Usually, she hosts a separate hour with just Hoda Kotb.

    She joined Hoda and Craig Melvin after she came back from Scotland, where she was supposed to interview Camilla, the queen consort, before the queen's death.

    "Joining us now is Jenna Bush Hager, just back from Scotland," Hoda started the segment.

    "So Jenna. You were set to interview, well, she's now Queen Consort, Camilla. And this was an interview that was a year in the making."

    She continued: "So, tell us why you were going. What was happening there and what went down."

    Jenna explained that they were working on it for a year and everyone was really excited.

    She went on to say that between her book club and Camilla's book club, they had a joint pick, so they decided to interview her.

    Jenna told her co-hosts that she had a dinner with King Charles III the night before he became king and told her that Camilla was really excited.

    ALL EYES ON HODA

    Jenna joining the panel comes as Hoda takes center stage on the show once again.

    Since Savannah has been overseas, the co-host has taken a seat in the middle and led many segments on her own.

    Usually, Savannah is in the middle and takes the spotlight away sometimes.

    Hoda has had the middle seat since Friday and also had a huge smile on her face, ever since Savannah left.

    Both of the women returned to the show last week, after Savannah took a bunch of time off to go on vacation with her family.

    While the mother-of-two was gone, Hoda took up the middle seat then as well.

    SHOUT OUT

    Last month, Jenna claimed that Hoda always yells at people on set to hurry up.

    During a segment of Today with Hoda and Jenna, she described her co-host as the “most disciplined”.

    Jenna said: “You’re always early. You’re yelling people to come on.

    "If you say we’re leaving at two, we’re really leaving at 1.30. That’s like my dad.”

    Hoda was heard chortling when her co-host tried to explain how the broadcaster resembled her dad.

    Jenna continued: “You like to get in and get out. In fact, if it’s time for other people to leave, you just turn off the lights. My dad does this.”

    Hoda admitted that she can be a “little controlling” and suggested the trait may influence her disciplined personality.

    She said: “I like to have my work out when I’m doing it. Not like ‘Hey, is so and so coming? Are they coming?’”

    Addressing viewers, Jenna said: “See? This is another reason why you’re [like] my dad.”

    Fans have gushed over the pair’s on-screen relationship.
