    SOPHIE Turner and Joe Jonas reportedly lived in an unhappy home before they split amid claims she has a partying lifestyle.

    Joe, 34, filed for divorce from the Game Of Thrones actress, 27, this week after four years of marriage.

    He stated in legal documents obtained by E! News: "The marriage between the parties is irretrievably broken."

    Sources have now claimed that the Cake By The Ocean singer never wanted to separate and that divorce was a "last resort."

    “Divorce was a last resort for Joe," an insider told Page Six.

    "He never wanted to break up his family, but he had to take what he felt was the best course of action for his girls."

    Joe and Sophie share two children - daughter Willa, three, and a one-year-old daughter whose name was set as "D.J." in divorce filings.

    The source went on: "An unhappy home isn’t a home, and the truth is that he and Sophie were going through it this year.

    "There’s a lot of noise out there, but it wasn’t a straw-that-broke-the-camel’s-back situation like it’s being reported.

    “It just kept building, and Joe finally reached a point where he felt that he had exhausted all options to salvage the marriage."

    Another source added to TMZ: "She likes to party, he likes to stay at home. They have very different lifestyles."

    SOLID PRENUP

    The famous pair - who married in 2019 - have a prenuptial agreement and Joe had been looking for a lawyer for at least a week.

    According to The Blast, the prenuptial agreement states that Joe will “keep all of his royalties from the music” with his siblings, while Sophie will keep the money she made from Game Of Thrones as well as “any residuals from her acting career.”

    The homes that they bought before getting married will remain with their rightful owner.

    Joe is said to be repped by Tom Sasser, who handled pro golfer Tiger Woods' infamous divorce from Elin Nordegren.

    MARRIAGE TROUBLES

    Over the weekend, fans were left stunned after a report claimed Joe and Sophie's marriage was rocky.

    At the time, insiders told TMZ the pair had been facing problems for at least six months.

    Sources also claimed that over the last three, Joe had been caring for their kids "pretty much all of the time."

    Joe, who resides in Los Angeles with Sophie, is currently on tour with his brothers Nick and Kevin - the outlet revealed their children are with him.

    Back in 2022, the British star admitted that she wanted to move back home to raise her little ones.

    "I miss England so much," she confessed to Us Weekly in an interview. "The people, the attitude, everything.

    "I'm slowly dragging my husband back. I really love living in America but, for my mental health, I have to be around my friends and my family."
