    Who is Marques Brownlee?

    Marques Brownlee aka Marques Keith Brownlee professionally known as MKBHD is an American rapper and professional ultimate frisbee player, He is most recognized for his impressive gadget review on the MKBHD YouTube channel.

    His channel is popular for its videos on technology including computers, smartphones, television, cameras, and other recently launched items.

    He had more than 20 million subscribers across all channels and 3.83 billion video views by August 2023. He and New York PoNY are the 2022 WFDF champions in the open category for ultimate frisbee.

    His YouTube channel is by far the most popular tech-related channel on the internet in terms of views and subscribers. He has also been featured on the official Time Magazine website.

    Brownlee went on to win the Shorty Awards for Decade’s best creator in 2018. He interviewed a number of celebrities including the CEO of Motorola, Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg, and Elon Musk. Marques released his Retro Tech his own unscripted series in 2019.
