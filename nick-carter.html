    Bio:
    Nick Carter (born Nickolas Gene Carter in Jamestown, NY on January 28, 1980) is a musician and member of the boy band, Backstreet Boys. As a kid, he auditioned for many acting roles and had a cameo in Edward Scissorhands. When Nick was 12, he joined the group, along with Brian Littrell, A.J. McLean, Kevin Richardson, and Howie Dorough. The band went on their first tour in 1995 and put out their first international album in 1996. The band continued their success until taking a two-year hiatus in 2001. Ever since 2003, they have still been a band. Nick released a solo album in 2002, and then paired up with Jordan Knight in 2014 for the Nick & Knight album. His family also had a TV show, House of Carters on E! in 2006, and he appeared on other shows like 8 Simple Rules and American Dreams. In 2014, he starred on his own reality show with his new wife, I Heart Nick Carter on VH1. He also wrote, directed and starred in a SyFy movie called, Dead 7. In 2014 he competed in Dancing With The Stars with partner Sharna Burgess, and came in second place. At the same time, he and wife Lauren Kitt announced they were pregnant with their first son.

    Best Known For:
    Nick Carter is best known for being part of the Backstreet Boys, and coming in second place on the 21st season of Dancing With The Stars.
