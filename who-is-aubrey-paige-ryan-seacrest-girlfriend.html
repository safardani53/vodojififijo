    Ryan Seacrest has been dating Aubrey Paige Petcosky since 2021.

    The TV personality was first linked to the model, more commonly known as Aubrey Paige, when they were spotted celebrating Memorial Day in the Hamptons in May 2021. They made their red carpet debut a year later.

    While Seacrest and Paige keep their relationship relatively private, the model has publicly supported her boyfriend on several occasions, including on his last day at Live with Kelly and Ryan in April 2023.

    Paige has also gotten to know several of Seacrest's celebrity pals, including Rita Ora and her husband, Taika Waititi. The foursome enjoyed a double date ahead of New Year's Rockin' Eve 2024.

    So, who is Ryan Seacrest's girlfriend? Here is everything to know about model Aubrey Paige.

    She's a model

    Paige has been modeling for a few years now. According to her Instagram bio, she is currently signed with Genetics Model Management.

    Her first big modeling campaign was with Dick's Sporting Goods. In August 2021, Paige posted photos from the campaign on Facebook, writing, "It means so much to have been able to work for a company that originated in my hometown Binghamton, NY. Check it out online, in stores and in the DSG catalog."
