    DIVAS' CHRISTMAS

    The Lifetime Original Movie, Ladies of the '80s: A Divas Christmas, premiered on December 2 at 8 pm on the cable network.

    The movie starred television icons Loni Anderson, 78; Morgan Fairchild, 73; Linda Gray, 83; Donna Mills, 82; and Nicollette.

    Fans will recognize Loni as Jennifer Marlowe from the CBS sitcom WKRP in Cincinnati, Morgan as her character Jordan Roberts from the CBS prime-time soap opera Falcon Crest, and Linda from Dallas, where she played Sue Ellen Ewing.

    Donna played the part of Abby Cunningham on Knots Landing and Nicollette portrayed Paige Matheson on the same show.

    Each of the Ladies of the '80s: A Divas Christmas character names is a play on the roles for which the actors became famous.

    Lifetime described A Divas Christmas as a story about "five glamorous ‘80s soap opera stars" who are forced to "share the spotlight" as they reunite to shoot the "final Christmas episode of their long-running soap opera."

    During the television shoot, old rivalries resurface and threaten to tear the production apart.

    The ladies' diva behavior almost cancels the show before it even begins, so they "reluctantly agree to set aside their differences and past secrets to 'act' as if they all still love each other.

    "Along the way, the divas also discover that the love between them all is still very strong."

    The network uploaded the trailer for the movie to its YouTube channel where fans rushed to comment on the upcoming release.
