    KASPER SCHMEICHEL is being linked with a shock move to Celtic that would see him reunite with former Leicester boss Brendan Rodgers.

    The Dane, 36, left the Foxes to join French side Nice 12 months ago after 11 years at the King Power Stadium.

    But earlier this week he saw his deal with the Ligue 1 club ripped up as the two parties went their separate ways.

    Now he is a free agent in search of a new challenge as he enters the latter stages of his career.

    Chelsea were linked with a move for the Premier League winner last month.

    But now, according to The Daily Record, Celtic are eyeing up the unemployed ace as potential competition for No1 Joe Hart.
