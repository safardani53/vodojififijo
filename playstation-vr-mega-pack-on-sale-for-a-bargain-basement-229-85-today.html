    28 Jan 2024, 00:00

    SONY has just released a new VR Mega Bundle, and you can already nab it for more than £70 off the RRP.

    ShopTo are offering the bundle, which normally sells for £299.99, for just £229.85, while Very are offering it for £229.99.

    The bundle gets you the headset, processor unit and camera, plus five games.

    • PlayStation VR Mega Pack, £229.99 from Very.co.uk (£199.99 for new customers with code N9MDQ) - buy it now
    • PlayStation VR Mega Pack, £229.85 from ShopTo.net - buy it now 

    It would be great value anyway, but that the games are almost universally excellent makes it a deal well worth snapping up.

    AstroBot Rescue mission is a brand-new VR only platform puzzler that is super-cute, challenging and fun.

    It also makes much better use of being in VR than you might expect for a platformer -- expect to be peering round corners, moving your head to hit in-game targets, and to see bots leaping out of your controller.

    There are also three classics ported to VR which are well worth your time.

    Skyrim VR is, as you might expect, all of The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim but playable in VR. So expect to be slaying dragons and casting spells in first person as you wander through the huge, gorgeous world.

    Bethesda's great Doom VR also gets thrown in for good measure, if you want something punchier and more intense.

    Then you've got the definitive WipEout Omega Collection for some zero-g racing with a pumping electronic soundtrack that really brings the PlayStation classic fully into the 21st century.

    Finally there's PlayStation VR Worlds that came with the previous VR bundle, which includes a whole host of little games and experiences to keep you entertained and show off the power of VR.

    All that, and you'll finally be ready for the sublime Tetris Effect -- and you'll be able to grab Borderlands VR when it comes out next week to boot.

    • PlayStation VR Mega Pack, £229.99 from Very.co.uk (£199.99 for new customers with code N9MDQ) - buy it now
    • PlayStation VR Mega Pack, £229.85 from ShopTo.net - buy it now
