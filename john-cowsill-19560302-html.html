    John Cowsill is an American musician known as the singer and drummer of the brother group The Cowsills. He is currently the drummer and singer for the touring group The Beach Boys, including originally Beach Boy Mike Love and longtime member Bruce Johnston. Cowsill also played keyboards for "Beach Boys Band", and according to the band's website, he took on vocals for the late Al Jardine and Carl Wilson. He also performed and recorded with Jan and Dean.
