    SHANIA Twain has shown off her figure in a leopard-print outfit, channeling a legendary look she wore 25 years ago.

    The 57-year-old singer recreated the ensemble from her music video for That Don't Impress Me Much.

    On Tuesday night, Shania wowed spectators as she arrived at the 2022 People's Choice Awards wearing an outfit reminiscent of the 1998 music video.

    She walked the red carpet rocking the iconic leopard-print look, consisting of a black velvet dress, a sheer bodice, and a bandeau.

    She also wore leopard-print fabric draped over her shoulders to mimic the cape she sported in the video.

    The Canada native completed her glam with a full face of makeup and her hair dyed a light shade of pink.

    Shania was presented with the Music Icon Award at the ceremony by Billy Porter, and she performed for a packed crowd.

    She stunned the audience with a couple of other sexy ensembles - one, a skintight pink suit topped with a pink cowgirl hat, and the second was a sparkly nude bodysuit.

    Fans went wild for the country star's sultry attire as a slew of comments poured in on Twitter over her look.

    One person commended her youthful appearance, tweeting: "Shania you are still as beautiful and sexy as ever."

    BACK ON TOUR

    The Grammy Award winner recently announced that she's heading back out on the road for her Queen of Me Tour.

    On November 3, 2022, Shania shared the news that she was hitting several cities to promote her forthcoming album Queen of Me.

    The tour reveal follows the release of Shania's song Waking Up Dreaming, the lead single from her Queen of Me album.
