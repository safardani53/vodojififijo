    Girl, two, dies after her family's car crashes on M1

    A TWO-YEAR-OLD girl has died after her familys car was involved in a horror crash with a lorry. The toddler, from Yorkshire, was in the vehicle with her family on the busy M1 near Nottingham on Wednesday.

    18 Jul 2024, 00:00

    A TWO-YEAR-OLD girl has died after her family’s car was involved in a horror crash with a lorry.

    The toddler, from Yorkshire, was in the vehicle with her family on the busy M1 near Nottingham on Wednesday.

    It happened at junction 25 at around 3pm and the road was closed by officers.

    The young girl was rushed to hospital but was pronounced dead shortly after arriving on Wednesday afternoon.

    A Nottinghamshire Police spokesman said: "The crash happened at around 3pm northbound between J26 and J27 on Wednesday.

    "A two-year-old girl was taken to hospital where she was pronounced dead shortly after arrival.

    "The young girl, from Yorkshire, was travelling with her family when their car was involved in a crash with a lorry.
