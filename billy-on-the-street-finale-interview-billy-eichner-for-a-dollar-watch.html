    Billy Eichner: Elena Is ‘Unbelievably Funny And Acerbic And Crazy’ On ‘Billy On The Street’

    We spoke to Billy at the American Comedy Awards on April 26 — which aired May 8 — and, of course, we asked about Elena, whom Billy described as “a real civilian [he] ran into that’s unbelievably funny and acerbic and crazy.” While we now have proof of Elena’s return, he told us at the time that “She’s the first person celebrity or non-celebrity that [he brings] to play “For A Dollar”, which … is a segment I usually do on my own.”

    Pretty sure there’s no greater honor than that, and if it were to be bestowed upon anyone, Elena is an obvious choice. She first appeared in the Season 2 premiere –which also featured Rashida Jones — and again in Season 3, directly alongside Lena Dunham. Elena totally stole the show each time.

    When Elena said “whertle” in her appearance with Lena Dunham, “it was the first time I ever broke,” Billy told us. (I couldn’t possibly explain it in words — you have to watch.) “Elena is just the funniest person. She’s so unpredictable. It’s pretty great.”

    Billy Eichner: ‘People Tweet At Me, ‘Will You Come To My Wedding And Scream At Me?”

    Because we’re totally professional journalists, we told Billy that our life goal is to get Billied On The Street, and we asked him his advice for viewers with a similar goal. “People come up to me and they’re like, ‘Where are you gonna be? I want you to scream at me.’ Very strange phenomenon,” he said. “People tweet at me, ‘Will you come to my wedding and scream at me?’ It’s like a thing, like I’m a bar mitzvah DJ now. I guess for the right price… No, I’m just kidding. I would not do that. It’s great to run into people on the street that know me, but they don’t make it onto the show, because it needs to be spontaneous.”

    So, basically, we need to pretend not to know him? “Well, I guess if you’re really good at pretending to not know me, you might make it on the show, but I can usually tell. People slip.” Unfortunately, Billy seems to have an excellent BS detector. But we’ll see, Billy. We’ll see.
