    The Game Raps About How Kim Kardashian Swallowed His Kids

    Yeezus Christ, you guys. The Game just went there. By which we mean the following: The Game just rapped in explicit, graphic, almost abusive detail about all the ways in which he used to have sex with Kim Kardashian.

    22 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Yeezus Christ, you guys.

    The Game just went there.

    By which we mean the following: The Game just rapped in explicit, graphic, almost abusive detail about all the ways in which he used to have sex with Kim Kardashian.

    At a listening party on Thursday night, the artist debuted an untitled song in which she delved deeply into his history with Mrs. Kanye West…

    … by talking all about how she used to take him deep. In her mouth, we mean.

    “I held Kim Kardashian by the throat, n-gga," he says at one point, according to a party attendee who recorded snippets of this track on video.

    "I made her swallow my kids until she choked, n-gga," he actually says at another point.

    To those of you who aren’t sure what this second line means, well… ask your friends or go use Google or something.

    Following these ridiculously intimate lines about Kardashian, The Game acknowledges her husband on the song and raps

    “I should apologize cause Ye my folks, n-gga."

    Even if he were serious, it somehow seems here like The Game is way past the point of an apology here. We doubt Kanye would accept.

    This isn’t the first time the musician has used his past with Kim and/or her sisters to garner headlines, either.

    He rapped that he “f-cked three Kardashians” in his song “Sauce" and then told Wendy Williams during an appearance on her talk show:

    “You know what, this is what I’ll say. I’ll tell you this. Kanye is a really good friend of mine.

    "And they got really, really beautiful kids, and I don’t want to disrespect their family.”

    Again, dude: We’re pretty sure it’s way too late for that.

    (For the record, meanwhile, The Game clarified to Williams that he had slept with Kim and Khloe and Blac Chyna, saying this back when the latter was expecting Rob Kardashian’s baby.)

    Last year, Kanye went off on Drake via Twitter merely because that artist followed his wife on Instagram.’

    West was simply aghast over this fact.

    “I never knew till this morning that Drake followed my wife on Instagram back in September,” he wrote in the a since-deleted tweet, adding at the time:

    "Imagine having a problem with somebody and they follow your wife on Instagram."

    That is hard to imagine.

    Even harder to imagine?

    How we’d react if a fellow rapper described to the world how he used to give it to our now-wife in various ways.

    Like, REALLY give it to our wife, you know?

    We feel like Kanye may end up having a thing or two dozen to say about this in the near future.
