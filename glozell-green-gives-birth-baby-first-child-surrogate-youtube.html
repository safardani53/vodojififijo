    It’s a GIRL! GloZell Green, 44, and her husband Kevin Simon welcomed their first child together, a precious daughter named O’Zell Gloriana De Green Simon, on Aug. 4, and already they are totally in love with their bundle of joy! But we don’t blame them, the adorable pair HAVE been wanting to become parents for quite a long time now.

    In fact, O’Zell was born via surrogate Shawna Johnson because GloZell couldn’t carry her own baby. And Shawna finally gave birth at 2:45 p.m., with little O’Zell weighing 8 lbs., 10 oz. and measuring 19.5 inches long. And as for the newborn’s super unique name? GloZell chose to honor her own family with her first child’s name as O’Zell was her late father’s name, Gloriana is a nod to her mother, Gloria, and De is for her sister, DeOnzell.

    “Shawna is doing great,” a rep for GloZell told People magazine. “GloZell and SK are overjoyed and their baby girl is healthy and adorable!” It doesn’t get much better than that! Kevin, also known as SK, were married back in 2013 and announced their happy news in December, gushing that they were “over the moon” about their child’s arrival.
