    TRIBUTES have been paid to a pub landlord after he was stabbed to death.

    Matthew Bryant was discovered with life-threatening injuries outside the Hare and Hounds, in Maidstone, Kent, following the horrific knifing.

    Cops rushed to the establishment, which Matthew managed, on Lower Boxley Road around 6pm on Monday.

    Paramedics administered medical treatment but Matthew was sadly pronounced dead shortly after.

    A woman, in her 50s, was arrested at the scene on suspicion of murder following the attack.

    Cops say she was known to Matthew.
