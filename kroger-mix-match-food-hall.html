    Kroger makes major shopping change - customers can 'mix and match'

    SELECT Kroger stores are introducing new sections in their stores, and it will give customers more options now. The few Krogers with these changes will have 10 new options for food shopping, allowing customers to mix and match their selections.

    09 Aug 2024, 00:00

    SELECT Kroger stores are introducing new sections in their stores, and it will give customers more options now.

    The few Krogers with these changes will have 10 new options for food shopping, allowing customers to mix and match their selections.

    Three Kroger stores in the Columbus, Ohio area will be opening food halls soon with a variety of options.

    Kroger is partnering with Kitchen United.

    The California-based company is behind the restaurants.

    Customers will have the option to order from their favorite restaurant or mix and match items for pickup or delivery.

    read more on Kroger

    Each supermarket location will carry six or more restaurants from the list below.

    1. Capriotti’s Sandwich Shop

    Capriotti’s Sandwich Shop serves cold, grilled, and vegetarian subs as well as cheese steaks and salads.

    Customers will be able to mix and match food from different restaurants.

    Most read in Money

    2. Genghis grill

    At Genghis grill, people can create their own stir-fried bowl with more than 80 fresh ingredients and flavorful sauces.

    Orders will be available for pickup or delivery through the digital kiosks, Mix Food Hall's app, or other third-party delivery platforms.

    3. Fuku

    Fuku has a variety of fried chicken options and sides inspired by Asian American David Chang.

    4. Hardee's

    Hardee’s is known for their Black Angus Thickburgers.

    5. Nathan's Famous

    Nathan's Famous is an American tradition serving New York favorites like hot dogs, burgers, milkshakes, and fries.

    6. Nekter Juice Bar

    Who doesn't like refreshing juice?

    Nekter Juice Bar makes juices, smoothies, açaí bowls without processed ingredients or artificial flavors.

    7. Pei Wei Asian Kitchen 

    Pei Wei Asian Kitchen is an Asian-inspired restaurant filled with handcrafted dishes on the menu.

    The restaurant has fresh, house-chopped veggies with whole cuts of white-meat chicken.

    They also have grass-fed steak with rice, noodle, or salad.

    8. Saladworks

    Everyone can do a salad or panini once in a while.

    Saladworks allows their customers to create their own salad with 60 fresh ingredient options.

    It's fresh all day, and you have the option to customize it to warm grain bowls or wraps.

    9. Wing Zone

    Wing Zone offers wings in 18 different flavors.

    They have four sections of flavor - buffalo, sweet & spicy, sweet no heat, and dry rub.

    In the Buffalo section, there are flavors like Tame, Louisiana Lemon Pepper, Garlic Parm, Buffalo Bliss, HotShot, Ragin' Cajun, and Nuclear Habanero.

    In the Sweet & Spicy section - Chipotle BBQ, Buffalo Ranch, Thai Chili, Smokin Q, and Mango Fire.

    In the Sweet No Heat section - Liquid Gold, Honey Q, and Sweet Samurai.

    Last but not least, Dry Rub has Lemon Pepper, Cool Ranch, and Blackened VooDoo.

    10. Wow Bao

    Wow Bao is another Asian-inspired restaurant that sells street food.

    This includes food like bao, pan-seared potstickers, steamed dumplings, and rice bowls.

    Which Kroger will carry the restaurants

    The Kroger located in Gahanna, Ohio will have Nathan’s Famous, Genghis Grill, Wow-Bao, Nekter Juice Bar, Fuku Fried Chicken, and Saladworks.

    The location in Dublin will have Capriotti’s Sandwich Shop, Wing Zone, Pei Wei Asian Kitchen, Nathan’s Famous, Nekter Juice Bar, and Fuku Fried Chicken.

    The last location in Clintonville will carry Hardee’s, Pei Wei Asian Kitchen, Wow-Bao, Nekter Juice, Saladworks, and Fuku Fried Chicken.

    Gahanna food hall will open on November 7, while the two other stores will open at the end of the month.
