    Early Life Of Juliette Binoche

    Juliette Binoche was born on 9 March 1964 in Paris to father Jean-Marie Binoche; an actor and director and mother Monique Yvette Staelens; an actress and teacher. She belongs to mixed ethnicity. Her old man has one-eighth Portuguese-Brazilian ancestry who was raised by French-born parents and her mother was born in Częstochowa, Poland.

In 1983, she finally played her first acting role and that was in the television series, Dorothée, danseuse de corde.
