    Inside Dancing On Ice's Michelle Heaton's 11th birthday party for daughter with Chanel-inspired cake

    DANCING On Ice star Michelle Heaton has thrown an impressive birthday party for her daughter Faith. The former Liberty X singer took to her Instagram to show off the fun day as she planned a Ninja Warrior-themed bash.

    23 Mar 2024, 00:00

    DANCING On Ice star Michelle Heaton has thrown an impressive birthday party for her daughter Faith.

    The former Liberty X singer took to her Instagram to show off the fun day as she planned a Ninja Warrior-themed bash.

    Michelle, 43, who is set to take part in the new series of Dancing On Ice this month, posted the snaps to Instagram.

    The mother-of-two proudly gushed over her daughter as she showed off her special day earlier this week.

    One snap saw Faith, 11, posing in front of her Chanel-inspired black and white birthday cake, topped with a flower.

    The youngster pulled her blonde hair back into a ponytail as she smiled wide wearing a pink 'Birthday Girl' sash.

    A second photo showed Faith and her friends posing for a photo in front of the entrance to Ninja Warrior UK Watford.

    While a third saw her cosied up to her pals and brother AJ, eight, at home around the dining table, which had been set up with treats.

    "My princess is turning 11 this coming week, and in true Heaton style it lasts a week," Michelle captioned the snaps.

    "I can’t believe how grown up you are. You are my best friend. Mummy & daddy are so proud of you. Our Beautiful, sassy, independent woman."

    Michelle went on to show further celebrations on her Instagram stories, including a visit to the Grove Hotel in Hertfordshire.

    Faith cosied up to her mum as she looked stylish in blue jeans and a black turtleneck, which she paired with white boots.

    Michelle let her brunette hair flow down her chest as she wore a zebra-print top and jeans for the special occasion.

    She went on to show her daughter being treated to a delicious plate of deserts as part of the birthday treat.

    It comes after Michelle posted photos of the shocking photos of the moment she collapsed and was rushed to hospital.

    The star opened up about her alcohol addiction last year in the shocking candid post.

    Michelle could be seen looking swollen and downcast in a hospital bed as she showed off the harsh reality of her addiction two years ago.

    Looking battered and bruised, the media personality shared the shocking snaps after she collapsed in "active addiction".

    One snap saw Michelle smiling in her hospital bed, while a second showed a close up of her eyes with heavy bruising.

    "2 years ago this was me... I was admitted to hospital, not for the first or the last time," she penned in the captioned.

    "I had collapsed in active addiction. I left the hospital the next day, I walked over to the off-licence and bought more vodka.

    "Seems insane? It is! But it was the only way I knew how to live. So I thought.

    "I swore to my peers, family and doctors I was on the mend. I meant it. But my mind of an addict had a different plan.

    "New year is a time we all make goals, and resolutions. Why don’t you make your goal to reach out to someone like me?

    "I turned it down so many times, so don’t be discouraged if they say no. The day will come when they’ve just had enough.

    "If you or anyone you know is struggling help is out there. One day at a time your life can be filled with hope faith & courage."
