    Yes, there is a Kylie Jenner sex tape out there.

    This is our takeaway from Jenner actually commenting on the rumor, at least.

    Ever since she split from Tyga, there’s been talk that the rapper would sell a sex tape he filmed with Kylie to the highest bidder.

    Considering the identity of her sister and the riches that followed the leaking of the Kim Kardashian sex tape, there’s every reason to believe an amateur porn really was filmed between Tyga and Jenner.

    And now Kylie herself has given us a new reason.

    Over the weekend, Kylie’s Twitter account was hacked, leading to a number of messages supposedly written by Jenner that included disses of Justin Bieber and other stars.

    It was pretty clear these Tweets did not come from Kylie.

    Especially because one of the Tweets mentioned the alleged sex tape she made with Tyga, as Kylie referred to it as “trash.”

    There was also the declaration of how Kylie “loves being so famous with no talent.”

    This does describe Kylie Jenner very well, but we somehow doubt it’s something Kylie Jenner would ever say.

    After recording one Snapchat video in which she responded by saying she doesn’t “really care” about the hacking and is just letting the moron who did it have “fun,” Kylie added a second video and said into the camera:

    “Everyone is like ‘leak the sex tape.'”

    “Guys, you are never going to see a sex tape from me. It’s not going to happen.”

    A video posted by Kylie Jenner Snapchats (@kylizzlesnapchats) on Jun 5, 2016 at 10:38pm PDT

    Ummm… that’s very different than saying there is no sex tape, wouldn’t you say?

    Moreover, watch the footage above. Is that really any kind of denial over the sex tape’s existence? Jenner is speaking in an almost seductive manner over the video.

    So that settles it. There’s no doubt in our mind that Kylie Jenner filmed herself having intercourse with Tyga.

    Go ahead and form your own conclusion. But just know you’re wrong if you think otherwise.
