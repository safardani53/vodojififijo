    PETER Andre has revealed he wants to get more tattoos, but his wife Emily "isn't into them".

    The Mysterious Girl singer - who has ink on both of his arms - has admitted he wants to follow in David Beckham’s footsteps and get more body art.

    But the 48-year star says Emily isn’t keen on the overly-tattooed look, so he he's put the idea on hold.

    Peter wrote in his New! magazine column today: "I didn't get my first tattoo until by 20s.

    "I went through my tattoo stage a while ago, and was going to carry on with them, but then I met Ems and she wasn't that into tattoos, so I fell out of the habit."

    Speaking about his own inking Pete said: "They're still subtle enough, they are not all over my body, but I think one or two more wouldn't go astray."

    He added: "So maybe in the future I might get more."

    Peter and Emily will mark their sixth wedding anniversary in July.

    The pair, who met in 2010, share daughter Amelia, seven, and son Theo, four.

    Meanwhile, earlier this month Peter revealed how he had refused to let his son Junior get a neck tattoo and pierced ears telling him: "Not on my watch."

    The 15-year-old, the singer's eldest son with ex-wife Katie Price, is plotting what he called a "glow up".

    He told Instagram followers: "When I’m 16 I hopefully will have a glow up. I want to get my two ears pierced.

    "I'm not sure I would suit just one and I don’t know which is the right one. When I’m older I want a neck tattoo as well - a big neck tattoo but on my face."

    "Don’t think I’d suit a sleeve you know, I dunno, but I’m thinking about a neck tattoo.

    "I want to do acting and you have to to cover them but yeah, i'm thinking about a neck tattoo."

    However, also writing in New magazine Peter said: "Someone told me he did a Q&A on Instagram and when he was asked how he feels about turning 16, somewhere in the answer he said he wants to get his ears pierced and a neck tattoo.
