    Who was Farrah Forke?

    Farrah Forke was an American actress from Corpus Christi, Texas.

    Born on January 12, 1968, Forke began her acting career at a young age.

    She entered the acting community with a role in a production of the musical The Rocky Horror Picture Show in Texas.

    She died at age 54 on February 25, 2022, in Texas from cancer.
