    A WOMAN has impressed homeowners by taking matters into her own hands when her bathroom sink got a giant crack. 

    Savvy Denise Isaacs, from Wales, fully restored the sink to new using a DIY repair kit which saved her hundreds of pounds. 

    Taking to the Facebook group Hinch Army House Decorating Inspiration And Tips, the homeowner said: “OMG!!! Look how I’ve mended my sink!!!!!

    “I’m going to start a new business!”

    She shared before and after photos of the large crack, after using a Cramer repair kit for enamel, acrylic and ceramic surfaces.

    Instead of forking out for a new sink, she was able to completely transform it to look like new by using the filling, sandpaper and spray in the kit. 

    Currently you can find the kit for around £24.99 on Amazon. 

    According to the listing: “The Cramer Bath and Kitchen Repair Kit is able to repair and restore damaged baths, sanitaryware and kitchen appliances by making near invisible and permanent repairs to chips and scratches.”

    People were highly impressed with her transformation and praised her efforts. 

    One said: “Well done who needs a man lol x”

    Another added: “Well done that’s brilliant!! You’d never know. You should be so chuffed. x”
