    Blake Lively may or may not have taken nude pics of herself, but she and Leonardo DiCaprio are looking like the real deal as couples go.

    The Gossip Girl starlet wore a knit smock and a face-concealing hat, while DiCaprio also kept things low key, donning jeans, windbreaker and a hat.
