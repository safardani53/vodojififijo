    16 Aug 2024, 00:00

    REWIND is a festival packed full of old school classics where organisers promise you can "party like it's 1989".

    Here's all you need to know if you're planning on splashing out on tickets to the music event and who is in the lineup...

    When is Rewind Festival 2018?

    The Rewind festival team are running three separate events to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the music weekender.

    You can attend the fun event in one of three locations:

    • Rewind Scotland, Perth - July 20-22
    • Rewind North, Cheshire - August 3-5
    • Rewind South, Henley-On-Thames - August 17-19

    How can I buy tickets to Rewind Festival 2018?

    Tickets are available online for each of the three festivals: Scotland, North and South.

    The price for each of the events is the same.

    The early bird and Tier two tickets are sold out and organisers are currently shifting the last batch of Tier three tickets.

    For adults who are camping for the weekend, tickets cost £135 plus a booking fee (which Rewind festival tells us is £10 per ticket).

    It's £115 + £10 booking fee for an adult not camping for the weekend. While day tickets are £65 plus that booking fee.

    Prices are cheaper for young people (between 10 and 17 years).

    It's £35 for a weekend camping ticket, £30 for a weekend without camping ticket and £20 for a day pass - all plus that £10 booking fee.

    Pre-payment plans are available foe the weekend camping and non-camping tickets.
