    Last night on Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 6, Nia Frazier took a bold stand in a move that would send shock waves rippling through the ALDC. How so?

    Watch Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 6 Online

    With Abby acting increasingly irrational, Holly asks the other Dance Moms to have her back on Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 6. Will they, though?

    Clearly she doesn’t trust them, and unloads on Abby for her treatment of Nia. Holly’s also been meeting in secret with Audrey about working together.

    She has an epic meltdown and leaves, and Abby immediately blames the girls with their poor showing, ripping their photos off the pyramid violently.

    The star again is Maddie Ziegler. If you watch Dance Moms online, you know:

    • The star, more often than not, is almost always Maddie Ziegler
    • Abby telling them they’re going to lose is a motivational tactic

    At rehearsal, Holly accuses Abby Lee Miller of trying to get rid of her like she did Kelly Hyland and Christi. For this week’s routine, Nia is out and JoJo is in.

    The girls reveal that JoJo took Nia’s slot. We see Nia and Holly meet with Aubrey O’Day, who hears about what has happened thus far with everything.

    Clearly, she’s taking a big risk two-timing the Abby Lee Dance Company, but it’s the sort of move one has to make to get ahead sometimes, it feels like.

    Meanwhile, JoJo is getting treated for head lice.

    The claws come out between Holly and Jess, with the former calling the latter a lousy mother because of the way she talked smack about Holly and Nia.

    JoJo gets fifth in the preteen division, while Kendall gets second in the same division. At the group awards, the team takes first place for their strong effort!

    Abby is so stoked and they celebrate … for now.

    Of course, Jill thinks Nia should’ve been in the routine, and looking ahead to next week, Abby seems a little too happy. She must be up to something …
