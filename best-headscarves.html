    MAKE no mistake: the headscarf is having a moment this season, so we've scoured the net to bring you the best headscarves for women for nailing this trend.

    When it comes to glamorous head-wear, there's no denying the retro romance of a silky headscarf - think Audrey Hepburn and her trademark babushka, or any given film scene where the heroine takes to the road in a convertible, styled with sunglasses and headscarf.

    The latter is such an iconic image in fact, that this generation's TikTokers have been flocking to create their very own 'headscarf on, sunroof down' moment, thanks to the Please Don't Go challenge. And yes, we can confirm that even canines look très chic wearing this trend.

    It's a timeless aesthetic that was also seen on the autumn/winter 2021 runways at both Dior and Versace, while music stars from Dua Lipa to ASAP Rocky have been spotted rocking theirs. So how to wear the headscarf? Women have been wearing them for centuries in a variety of ways: try yours tied at the nape of the neck, bandana style à la Y2K, or rolled into a chic headband to keep your hair out of your face in the summer.

    This season there's an abundance of prints, colours and fabrics to try out: from Pucci-eque floral prints and classic silky scarves to on-trend chequerboard, kashmir prints and paisley.

    Take a look below at our pick of the best headscarves for women available to shop now.
