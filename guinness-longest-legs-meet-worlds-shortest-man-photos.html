    Guinness Longest Legs Meet World's Shortest Man (Photos)

    Svetlana Pankratova currently holds the Guinness World Record for the woman with the longest legs. She recently met Chinas He Pingping. According to the Guinness Book of Records, He Pingping is the worlds shortest man.

    14 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Svetlana Pankratova currently holds the Guinness World Record for the woman with the longest legs. She recently met China’s He Pingping. According to the Guinness Book of Records, He Pingping is the world’s shortest man.

    The photo op took place yesterday at London’s Trafalgar Square. Pankratova’s legs measure 132 cm or 4.33 feet. She is 6’7″ and is not the tallest woman. That distinction was until recently held by Sandy Allen who was 7’7″. She died at age 53 last month. He Pingping, 20, is 2’5″.
