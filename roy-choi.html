    Roy Choi Biography

    Roy Choi was born on February 24, 1970, to Soo Myung Choi and Jai Nam Choi. His parents met in the United States but married in Korea.     Career

    In the mid-1970s, Roy Choi established himself as a mid-land hotel chef. He began working for Hilton Hotels in 2001. He was promoted to Chef De Cuisine in 2007. He met his partner, Mark Manguera, there. He also worked at Sacramento’s Embassy Suites and Los Angeles’ Rock Sugar Pan Asian Kitchen.

    Following his classical training, he moved on to food trucks on Venice’s About Kinney Boulevard. He rose to prominence as the founder of Kogi in 2008. He was named one of the Best New Chefs 2010 in 2010. He currently owns and operates Sunny Spots in Venice, California, which are Caribbean-inspired. He opened Best Friend, a restaurant in Las Vegas, in December 2018. His cooking style combines Mexican and Korean elements.
