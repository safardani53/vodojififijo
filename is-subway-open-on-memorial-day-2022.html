    MEMORIAL Day is one of the ten nationally recognized federal holidays closing many stores and businesses nationwide.

    This year Memorial Day lands on Monday, May 30, 2022.

    Is Subway open on Memorial Day 2022?

    Subway will remain open for normal business hours on Memorial Day serving its popular submarine sandwiches.

    The sandwich shop only closes its doors on Christmas Day.

    You can still get any of their 13 signature Subway sandwiches or customize your own 364 days a year.

    The fast-food restaurant has more than 46k locations around the globe and roughly 21k stores in the US.

    What is closed on Memorial Day?

    Because Memorial Day is a federal holiday many businesses close their doors for the day.

    The Federal Reserve Bank is closed meaning most bank branches will also not be operating.

    Other public institutions such as the United States Postal Service, public schools, public libraries, and the Department of Motor Vehicle will be closed.

    What is Memorial Day?

    Memorial Day is celebrated in honor of military members who died serving their country.

    It was first established after the Civil War when Americans lost 620k soldiers on both sides of the Union and Confederate.

    The holiday was originally called Decoration Day and became recognized as Memorial Day when it became an official federal holiday in 1971.
