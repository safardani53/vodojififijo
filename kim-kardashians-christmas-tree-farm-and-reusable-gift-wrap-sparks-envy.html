    Kim Kardashian is coming through with the excessive Christmas decorations this year. It’s no secret the Kardashian-Jenner family members get quite competitive during holidays when it comes to decor. The sisters are often battling it out for who can put on the most impressive display. Judging by fans’ reactions, Kim Kardashian’s winter wonderland-style Christmas tree hallway is making everyone green with envy.

    She may be a mom-of-four, a budding lawyer, a reality TV star, and a billionaire business owner, but nothing is going to stop Kim Kardashian when it comes to her Christmas aesthetic. This year, her elf-on-the-shelf scenes are sending shockwaves across the internet. Plus, the 43-year-old is playing into environmentalists’ hands this festive season as she shows off her “reusable” SKIMS gift wrap.

    Kim Kardashian’s Christmas tree hallway

    Yes. You read that right, Kim Kardashian is showing off not just a standard tree, but a Christmas tree hallway this holiday period. There are so many in her home that it’s as if she has her own Christmas tree farm!

    Taking to her Instagram Stories just days before December 25, Kim shared a video of her hallway filled with snow-topped Christmas trees. At least 13 trees can be seen in the clip as the Hulu star gives fans a preview of her decorated home.

    The Kardashians star’s “white theme” and tree-filled home has clearly gone down well with some of her fans.

    One person tweeted via X (Twitter): “People are trashing on Kim Kardashian for her Christmas tree hallway but like? I want a christmas tree hallway. People hate on her cause she is so successful and has the life so many dream of. Who cares where she got her money. Back to the trees LOOK AT THEM I LOVE IT!”

    View Tweet

    Kim’s fills her tub with cocoa

    The same day Kim shared her stunning hallway filled with Christmas trees, she also gave her followers an insight into the lengths she goes to with her kids‘ elf on the shelf.

    With four young kids, she clearly has to keep them all entertained, and judging by the reality star’s Story she’s pulling out all the stops.

    Kim walks into her bathroom and zooms in on the “wild” elf on the shelf set up which sees her bathtub filled with cocoa and lots of mischievous elf figurines getting up to no good.

    View Instagram Post

    Christmas decorations keep coming

    Kim’s creative genius doesn’t stop at a cocoa bath, however. The SKIMS founder is taking her Christmas decorations to new heights once again as she unveils this year’s gift wrap.

    Sharing a video of her presents in front of her heavily lit main Christmas tree, Kim says: “I used SKIMS cotton fabric for my wrapping this year and I’m going to reuse it.”

    The star’s box-shaped gifts are all wrapped in white SKIMS jersey material as they sit under the tree.

    She adds: “I love how this turned out. Everything wrapped in SKIMS cotton jersey so we can reuse it. I went with winter white this year. That’s just the theme of the winter wonderland.”
