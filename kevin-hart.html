    Comedian, actor, and entrepreneur Kevin Hart got his start by winning several amateur comedy competitions. He was cast in Judd Apatow's TV series Undeclared and went on to act in several other comedies including Scary Movie 3 and Soul Plane.

    Kevin got his big break after the release of his comedy special I'm a Grown Little Man in 2009. He followed it up with two more successful specials: Seriously Funny (2010) and Laugh at My Pain (2011).

    Kevin has gone on to star in a slew of comedy films such as Think Like a Man, Ride Along, Get Hard, and Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle. Along with movies, Kevin also co-created the TV show Real Husbands of Hollywood, where he starred alongside Nick Cannon and Robin Thicke.

    Along with acting, Kevin also launched his production company HartBeat Productions and global media company Laugh Out Loud, which is known for online content such as Cold as Balls and What the Fit.

    Kevin is a proud father and has four kids: Heaven Leigh, Hendrix, Kenzo Kash, and Kaori May.
